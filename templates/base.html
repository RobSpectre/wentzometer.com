What's this jawn?

When the Philadelphia Eagles traded Carson Wentz to the Indianapolis Colts in February, the Eagles received two draft picks. The first was for the 85th overall this year, the next a second round pick for 2022, but with one condition.

If Carson Wentz plays 75% of the total offensive snaps of the season or 70% of the total offensive snaps and the Colts make the playoffs, the Eagles will receive a first round draft pick.

This website and accompanying Twitter bot tracks Carson Wentz's snaps to see if the Philadelphia Eagles will indeed get a first round draft pick in 2022.
